About Us

Perched on a peaceful hilltop in Calicut, Samava Farms is a little slice of paradise wrapped in nature's embrace. The name "Samava" means peace and serenity—exactly what you'll feel the moment you step in.

This cozy retreat is surrounded by lush greenery, crisp mountain air, and breathtaking views. Within the property, you'll find a charming farm and a small plantation garden, adding to the natural charm.

Looking for a movie night with family or friends? The upstairs space is perfect! It's a cozy spot with an Arabic Majlis, where you can sink into soft cushions, watch a film, or lose yourself in a good book.

But the real magic? Sunrise. From the terrace, you get a breathtaking view of Calicut Beach in the distance, bathed in golden light. A moment worth waking up for.

Samava Farms isn't just a stay; it's an experience. A place to unwind, reconnect, and soak in nature's beauty. 🌿✨
