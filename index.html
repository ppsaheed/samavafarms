A Serene Retreat in the Hills of Calicut

About Samava Farms

Named after “Samava,” which embodies peace and serenity, our farm is a mountaintop retreat designed to offer a tranquil and rejuvenating experience. Nestled in a serene hilltop in Calicut, this farm property is a natural bliss, surrounded by lush greenery, fresh mountain air, and breathtaking views. Whether you seek a peaceful escape, a family gathering, or a private celebration, Samava Farms is your perfect destination.

The Experience

At Samava Farms, we offer luxurious living blended with the charm of nature. Our three-floor villa is adorned with Moroccan-inspired interiors, making it an ideal location for photoshoots. The open mountain landscape and uninterrupted breeze create a soothing environment, perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Accommodation & Amenities

  • Standard Room: Features 1 Double Bed or 2 Twin Beds, two bedside tables, a desk, and a chair. Elegantly furnished with wall-to-wall carpeting, stylish interiors, and a private balcony to enjoy the stunning scenery.
  • Ultra-modern Glass Bathroom: Equipped with a hairdryer, magnifying shaving and makeup mirror, and all essential amenities for a comfortable stay.
  • Exclusive Welcome Perks: Enjoy a complimentary bottle of wine, fresh fruit, and mineral water upon arrival.
  • Facilities:
    • Wind & Weather: The mountain breeze is a unique experience, making this an enchanting stay.
    • Upstairs Event Hall: Ideal for family gatherings, musical evenings, Ghazals, and dance practices.
    • Total Privacy: With no neighboring properties, you’ll experience undisturbed peace.
    • 24/7 Security & Assistance: A dedicated team member is on-site round the clock to ensure your safety and assist whenever needed.

Our Vision – A Commitment to Sustainability

At Samava Farms, we are dedicated to preserving nature and sustainability. Our farm is designed to conserve greenery and protect the environment, ensuring a harmonious coexistence with nature. Every effort is made to maintain the natural ecosystem while providing a luxurious yet eco-friendly retreat.

Location & Accessibility

  • 10 km from Calicut town – A short and scenic drive from the heart of the city.
  • 35 km from Calicut International Airport – Easily accessible for domestic and international travelers.
  • Perfect for a quick getaway in Kerala – Whether you live nearby or are visiting, this is your ideal escape.
